The `+coinflip` command flips a virtual coin. It can be used for fun or to decide between two options.

## Usage
- `+coinflip` – flips a coin and returns **heads** or **tails**.
- `+coinflip bet @user amount` – initiates a bet with another user. Both participants must have at least the specified amount of **sonhos**. The winner receives the total bet.

## Fairness
The result is generated from a secure random source, guaranteeing a 50 % chance for each side. No user can influence the outcome.
