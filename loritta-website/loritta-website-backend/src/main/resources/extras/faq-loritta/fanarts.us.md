### But attention!
Depending on how you make your fanart, it is good to follow some tips, to deliver a fanart worthy of rewards!

#### <img src="https://cdn.discordapp.com/emojis/528266482795151371.png?v=1" class="inline-emoji"><img src="https://abs.twimg.com/emoji/v2/72x72/1f4f1.png" class="inline-emoji"> Tips for Digital Arts <img src="https://cdn.discordapp.com/emojis/528266482795151371.png?v=1" class="inline-emoji"><img src="https://abs.twimg.com/emoji/v2/72x72/1f4f1.png" class="inline-emoji">
* The drawing must be in png! Never use jpg for digital drawings, jpg was made for photos and loses quality in digital drawings!
* If your drawing doesn't have a customized "background" (that is, has white/transparent background), cut the image so that only the drawing stays!
Examples of Digital Arts:
![Drawing by Its_Gabi](https://loritta.website/assets/img/fanarts/Loritta_-_Its_Gabi.png)

#### <img src="https://abs.twimg.com/emoji/v2/72x72/1f4f7.png" class="inline-emoji"> Tips for Arts on Paper<img src="https://abs.twimg.com/emoji/v2/72x72/1f4f7.png" class="inline-emoji">
* Utilize jpg to save space!
* The photo of the drawing must be decent, that is: With good lighting, focused correctly and rotated in the correct position; The clearer and easier to be visualized, better!

### Resuming...
* Send the image on Discord (instead of sending the image on other websites and then sending the link in the channel)
* We want proofs (screenshots of the screen, sketch of the drawing, photo of the PC screen, etc.) that it was you who made the drawing, send photos of the art process together with the drawing! <img src="https://cdn.discordapp.com/emojis/626942886583205898.png?v=1" class="inline-emoji">
Examples of Proofs:
![Proof in GIF or video](/v3/assets/img/faq/fanarts/heathecliff_hope.gif)
![Proof in screenshot (Showing the layers)](/v3/assets/img/faq/fanarts/heathecliff_process.png)

Remember that this takes a time and the only bot here is Loritta, for the rest we are humans who many times are busy and have other responsibilities in our day-to-day! If you stay insisting, you will be punished for "begging for the artist role". <img src="https://cdn.discordapp.com/emojis/741058240455901254.png?v=1" class="inline-emoji">
* Make your art with affection! <img src="https://cdn.discordapp.com/emojis/412482280548728833.gif?v=1" class="inline-emoji"> Remember that it will be posted on the site! ## Reference Sheet
And not only forget the main element! The **Loritta's reference sheet**, since she is a character, she must come accompanied by her classic outfit lol.

While Lori loves using her white hoodie, she also has a black version of the hoodie.

The lines of her hoodie (the ones that stay on the arm) possess a lighter line and a darker one, while the details of the hoodie (collar, etc) are an intermediate blue tone between the two colors.

There exist these two different colors to demonstrate the balance between what Lori does: The light blue represents joy and peace, the dark blue represents moderation and justice.

Currently we have two main **Ref Sheets** about Loritta, one of colors and another of her outfits, you can use them to obtain the correct coloration of Loritta's colors. **Do not use the Ref Sheet with principles for Tracing or anything of the type, we are not going to allow actions of the type, and you can be banned from Loritta if you try to plagiarize or trace some artist of hers.**
